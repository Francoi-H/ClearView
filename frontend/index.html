How it Works

ClearView combines deep-learning AI detection with C2PA Provenance technology. While the AI looks for pixel patterns, C2PA checks for secure digital signatures that verify an image's origin.

Limitations:
  • AI detection is probabilistic; it can have false positives.
  • C2PA metadata is often stripped by social media platforms.
  • Lack of C2PA data does not automatically mean an image is fake.
