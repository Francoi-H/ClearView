What is ClearView?

ClearView is an experimental AI image detection tool designed to help identify whether an image may have been generated or heavily altered by artificial intelligence.

The system analyzes visual patterns learned from real and AI-generated images and returns a probability score along with a human-readable prediction.

How to read the results

  • P(AI) represents the model’s estimated probability (0–1) that an image is AI-generated.
  • Confidence reflects how strongly the model supports the final prediction label.
  • A higher P(AI) indicates stronger evidence of AI generation.

Limitations

ClearView is intended for research and educational use only. Results may be affected by image quality, compression, editing, or unseen AI models. Predictions are not guaranteed.
